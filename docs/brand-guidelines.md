# Brand Guidelines

## Brand Name

Spiritual Peach

## Mission

To create gentle, accessible spiritual tools that help people reconnect with their intuition, self-worth, healing, and personal growth.

## Brand Values

* Compassion over perfection
* Curiosity over fear
* Growth over shame
* Self-trust over external validation
* Gentle consistency over intensity

## Brand Personality

### We Are

* Warm
* Encouraging
* Playful
* Mystical
* Grounded
* Supportive

### We Are Not

* Fear-based
* Dogmatic
* Elitist
* Pushy
* Judgmental
* Overly "woo"

## Tone of Voice

Write as if speaking to a trusted friend.

### Preferred Language

* Gentle
* Empowering
* Clear
* Accessible

Examples:

* "You are allowed to grow at your own pace."
* "Take what resonates and leave the rest."
* "Trust your inner wisdom."

### Avoid

* Fear tactics
* Guaranteed outcomes
* Spiritual superiority
* Manipulative sales language

## Visual Direction

### Primary Colors

* Soft Peach
* Warm Cream
* Moonlight White

### Accent Colors

* Rose Gold
* Soft Lavender
* Cosmic Blue

### Imagery

Use:

* Clouds
* Moons
* Stars
* Soft gradients
* Light
* Nature
* Sacred geometry (minimal)

Avoid:

* Horror imagery
* Dark occult symbolism
* Heavy gothic themes
* Overly busy compositions

## Inspiration

* Headspace
* Calm
* Notion
* Modern wellness brands
* Soft celestial aesthetics

## Core Promise

Spiritual growth can be gentle, beautiful, and approachable.
