# 🍑 Spiritual Peach

A modern spiritual wellness platform focused on self-discovery, healing, mindfulness, and personal growth.

Spiritual Peach combines oracle cards, guided meditations, affirmations, rituals, and educational content into a beautiful digital experience designed to help people reconnect with their intuition and create lives that feel aligned and intentional.

---

## Vision

Create a welcoming digital sanctuary where visitors can:

* Explore spiritual practices without overwhelm
* Build self-awareness and confidence
* Access guided meditations and affirmations
* Discover rituals for reflection and personal growth
* Connect with a supportive community
* Find tools that encourage intentional living

---
